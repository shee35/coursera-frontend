Three stages of human life

Childhood
Childhood is the age span ranging from birth to adolescence. Childehood contains two stages :preoperational stage and operational stage. It is the time of children to be in school and at play, to grow strong and confident with the love and encouragement of their family.It is a precious time in which children should live free from fear, safe from voilence and protected from abuse and exploitation.
Teenage
A teenager, or teen, is a person who falls within the ages of 13 to 18 years old. The word "teenager" is often associated with adolescence. The teenage years can be difficult for parents and kids alike. The combination of hormonal changes, new academic and social oppotunities and responsibilities does not always end up a good combination for all.
Adulthood
Adulthood, the period in the human lifespan in which full physical and intellectual maturity have been attained. Adulthood is commonly thought of as beginning at age 20 or 21 years. Middle age, commencing at about 40 years, is followed by old age at about 60 years.
